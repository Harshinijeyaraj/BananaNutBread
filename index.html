Here is a step by step instruction to make a really good bannana nut bread.

Ingredients you need are...

4 large ripe bananas.

2 cups of all purpose flour

2 teaspoon baking soda

1 cup butter

2 cups of white sugar

4 eggs

1 cup of walnuts/almonds

First peel the bananas and mash them up

Then, combine the flour

Add in the baking soda and salt and mix it well

Then, take a separate bolow and cream together the sugar and butter

Add the creamed butter and sugar mixture to the banana and flour mixture and mix.

add the 4 eggs and mix well

Add the almonds and mix one last time

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees farenhite

Then take a baking pan and grease it. Then cut line the pan with parchment paper

Bake it for 1 hour/60 minutes. Once finished, take it out, let it cool for a few minutes and enjoy!
